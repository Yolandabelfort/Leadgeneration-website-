Privacy Policy

Last updated July 4, 2025

This privacy notice for BeautyCommerce Ltd (trading as WeGenerateHomeImprovementLeads) (“we”, “us”, or “our”) explains how and why we collect, store, use, and share (“process”) your personal information when you use our services (“Services”), such as when you visit https://WeGenerateHomeImprovementLeads.com or engage with us in sales, marketing, or events.

Questions? Contact info@beautycommerce.co.uk. If you do not agree with this notice, please do not use the Services.

Summary of Key Points

  • Data we process: details you provide (name, email, phone, service interests) and technical data collected automatically (IP, device, usage).
  • Sensitive data: we do not process sensitive categories.
  • Why we process: to operate and improve the Services, communicate with you, prevent fraud/security issues, and comply with law.
  • Sharing: with trusted service providers/affiliates under contracts, or when required by law or during business transfers.
  • Security: administrative, organizational, and technical safeguards are in place, but no system is 100% secure.
  • Your rights: depending on your location, access, rectification, deletion, restriction, objection, and portability.

1) Information We Collect

Personal information you provide

When you request information, submit a form, or contact us, we collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, company, and service interests. All information you provide must be accurate and kept up-to-date.

Information collected automatically

We automatically collect device and usage data, including IP address, device/browser type, operating system, language, referring URLs, and interactions with our site. We use cookies and similar technologies for analytics, security, and site functionality. You may manage cookies via your browser settings; some features may not function without them.

2) How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Services and our lead delivery.
  • To communicate (service messages and, where permitted, marketing).
  • To protect against fraud, misuse, and security incidents.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce agreements.

3) Legal Bases (UK/EU GDPR)

We process personal data under one or more of the following bases: consent, contract (to deliver requested services), legitimate interests (e.g., improving Services), and legal obligation.

4) Sharing Your Information

We share data with service providers (hosting, analytics, communication), affiliates of BeautyCommerce Ltd, professional advisers, and authorities where required. All vendors are bound by confidentiality and data processing terms.

5) Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as required by law. When no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it, or securely store and isolate it until deletion is possible.

6) Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. Despite our efforts, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7) Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18 years of age.

8) Your Rights

Where applicable (e.g., UK/EU), you can request access, rectification, deletion, restriction, portability, or object to processing. To exercise rights, contact info@beautycommerce.co.uk. You may lodge a complaint with the ICO (UK) or your local authority.
