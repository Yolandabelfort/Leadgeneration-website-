Data Policy

Last updated July 4, 2025

Scope

This Data Policy explains our practices regarding personal information processed by BeautyCommerce Ltd (trading as Beautycommerceltd) in connection with lead generation for home services (Roofing, Windows, Gutters, Garden). This policy is directed to users in the United States and reflects U.S. privacy law requirements.

Data We Collect

  • Contact data — name, email, phone, company, role.
  • Lead preferences — service vertical, location, budget, timeline.
  • Usage/technical — IP address, device/browser, pages viewed, timestamps, referral URLs, cookie identifiers.

Purposes of Processing

  • Supply, manage, and improve our Services.
  • Deliver and verify leads; prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Customer support and account communications.
  • Compliance with legal/regulatory obligations.
  • Marketing communications (where permitted and subject to your preferences).

U.S. Privacy Framework (CCPA/CPRA & similar state laws)

We process personal information in accordance with applicable U.S. laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (as amended by the CPRA) and similar state privacy laws (e.g., Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Utah). Depending on your state of residence, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to know/access the categories and specific pieces of personal information collected.
  • Right to delete personal information, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information and of targeted advertising.
  • Right to portability and non-discrimination for exercising your rights.

We do not sell personal information for money. Certain analytics/advertising uses may be considered “sharing” under state law; you can opt out by contacting us.

Sharing & Processors

We use vetted vendors for hosting, analytics, messaging, CRM/webhooks, payment processing, and security. Each processor is bound by confidentiality and data processing terms. We may disclose information to professional advisers or authorities when required by law, and in connection with business transfers (e.g., merger, acquisition).

Cross-Border Data Transfers

We may process and store information in the United States and other countries. Where required by applicable law, we implement contractual and other safeguards for cross-border transfers.

Retention

Personal information is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law. When no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it, or securely store and isolate it until deletion is possible.

Security

We implement organizational and technical measures (access controls, encryption in transit, least-privilege, monitoring). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights (U.S.)

Depending on your state, you may request access, deletion, correction, portability, or opt out of sale/sharing and targeted advertising. To exercise rights, email info@Beautycommerceltd.com. We may need to verify your identity and can accept authorized-agent requests where permitted. If we deny your request, you may appeal by replying to our decision with “Appeal” in the subject line.
