Data Policy

How we collect, use, retain, and safeguard data.

Scope

This Data Policy explains our practices regarding personal data processed by BeautyCommerce Ltd (trading as WeGenerateHomeImprovementLeads) in connection with lead generation for home services (Roofing, Windows, Gutters, Garden).

Data We Collect

  • Contact data — name, email, phone, company, role.
  • Lead preferences — service vertical, location, budget, timeline.
  • Usage/technical — IP address, device/browser, pages viewed, timestamps, referral URLs, cookie identifiers.

Purposes of Processing

  • Supply, manage, and improve our Services.
  • Deliver and verify leads; prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Customer support and account communications.
  • Compliance with legal/regulatory obligations.
  • Marketing communications (where permitted and subject to your preferences).

Lawful Bases (UK/EU)

We rely on one or more of: consent, contract, legitimate interests, and legal obligation.

Sharing & Processors

We use vetted vendors for hosting, analytics, messaging, CRM/webhooks, payment processing, and security. Each processor is bound by data processing agreements. We may disclose data to professional advisers or authorities when required, and during business transfers.

International Transfers

Where data is transferred outside the UK/EEA, we implement appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses and supplementary measures.

Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for the above purposes or as required by law. When no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it, or securely store and isolate it until deletion is possible.

Security

We implement organizational and technical measures (access controls, encryption in transit, least-privilege, monitoring). No method is 100% secure.

Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability. To exercise rights, email info@beautycommerce.co.uk. You may lodge a complaint with the ICO (UK) or your local authority.
